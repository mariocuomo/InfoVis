INFORMATION VISUALIZATION PROJECT

multidimensional icons to encode multivariate data

The goal of Multidimensional Data Visualizations is to encode multidimensional data into graphic objects with characteristics that depend on the features of interest.
In the example we have 10 datacases. Each datacase has 4 positive quantitative variables and an identifier - which for simplicity is a character.
Each datacase is represented with a house and the 4 quantitative variables x, y, z, k are encoded respectively to the height, width, height of the roof and garage's width.
You can sort the datacases according to the feature you prefer: just left-click on it.
